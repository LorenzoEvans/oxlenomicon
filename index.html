  • Purpose
    The purpose of this site is to explore the DAT protocol, Hashbase, and Beaker ecosystem, while also experimenting with additional combinable decentralized web platforms, tools, and technologies, to explore the idea of a memex, or eco-cortex (no idea where I heard that, but I don't *think* I came up with it), and to see what lies at the intersection of a web-site, and a living piece of digital art.
    Perhaps, even deeper, the purpose of this site is also to explore, track, and observe my very self, as if I were an external entity, to see what the self gravitates toward consuming and creating, expressing, an odd mirror, if you will, though not the fun-house variety of odd-ness, hopefully.
